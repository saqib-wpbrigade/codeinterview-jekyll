Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer free trials?

Yes, we offer a 10-day free trial and you can test our code editor free of charge without registering an account.

Do you have monthly and yearly billing options?

Yes, you can choose a yearly subscription or a monthly one. Choosing the yearly will save you about 10%.

How do team accounts work?

You can work along with your team to prepare and conduct interviews. Your teammates share the same pads and snippets as you. They can also create pads or snippets, which you can see too.

How long are my interviews stored for?

We keep your data forever. This includes playback too! You can always come back and review each moment.

Can I cancel at any time?

Yes, there is no minimum contract with Code Interview. You can cancel at any time without penalty.

Do you offer refunds?

If you cancel within 30 days of starting your plan, we'll refund you in full in accordance with our refund policy.
